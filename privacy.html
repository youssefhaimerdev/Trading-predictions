1. Overview

APEX Intelligence ("we," "our," or "us") operates the APEX Intelligence trading analysis platform (the "Service") at apex-intelligence.vercel.app. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our Service.

APEX Intelligence is a client-side application. This means the vast majority of processing occurs directly in your browser. We do not operate backend servers that store your personal trading queries, analysis results, or browsing behavior within the app itself.

ℹ Key principle: APEX Intelligence is designed to be privacy-first. We do not sell your personal data. We do not create personal profiles. All AI analysis runs locally in your browser.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

APEX Intelligence does not require account registration and does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or financial account details. You can use the full platform anonymously.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, certain information may be automatically collected by our hosting infrastructure (Vercel) and any analytics tools we employ:

  • IP Address: Collected by Vercel's infrastructure for security and DDoS protection purposes. We do not store or process IP addresses for marketing.
  • Browser Type and Version: Used to ensure compatibility and improve the platform experience.
  • Device Type: Desktop, mobile, or tablet — used for responsive design optimization.
  • Pages Visited and Time on Page: Aggregated and anonymized usage statistics.
  • Referring URL: The website that referred you to APEX Intelligence.
  • Timestamps: When you accessed the Service.

2.3 Information NOT Collected

We explicitly do not collect or store:

  • Which assets you analyze or search for
  • Your trading decisions or portfolio information
  • Your financial situation or investment goals
  • Personally identifiable information without your consent
  • Credit card or payment information (the Service is free)

3. Third-Party APIs & Services

APEX Intelligence integrates with several third-party data providers to deliver real-time market data. When you use the Service, your browser may make requests to these external services. Each has its own Privacy Policy:

3.1 Binance (crypto.binance.com)

We connect to Binance's public WebSocket streams and REST API to fetch real-time cryptocurrency prices and historical OHLCV data. These are public, unauthenticated endpoints. No personal information is sent to Binance. See: binance.com/en/privacy

3.2 CoinGecko (coingecko.com)

We use CoinGecko's free public API for cryptocurrency market data including prices, 24-hour changes, and market capitalization. No personal data is transmitted. See: coingecko.com/en/privacy

3.3 Alternative.me (alternative.me)

We use the Alternative.me Fear & Greed Index API for crypto market sentiment data. This is a public API requiring no authentication. No personal data is transmitted.

3.4 Frankfurter (frankfurter.app)

We use the Frankfurter ECB exchange rate API for foreign exchange (Forex) data. This is a completely open API with no authentication. No personal data is transmitted.

3.5 Yahoo Finance (via AllOrigins proxy)

We fetch stock market data through Yahoo Finance's public endpoints via the AllOrigins CORS proxy service. Your IP address may be visible to both Yahoo Finance and AllOrigins when making these requests. See: finance.yahoo.com/privacy

3.6 Metals.live (metals.live)

We use metals.live for spot gold and silver price data. This is a public API. No personal data is transmitted.

3.7 RSS News Feeds

We fetch RSS feeds from MarketWatch, CoinDesk, Yahoo Finance, and CryptoPanic via the AllOrigins proxy. No personal information is included in these requests.

3.8 Google Fonts

We use Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com) to load web fonts. Google may collect certain data about font requests. See: policies.google.com/privacy

3.9 Lightweight Charts (TradingView)

The charting library is loaded from jsDelivr CDN. No personal data is transmitted to TradingView or jsDelivr beyond standard CDN request headers.

3.10 Vercel (Hosting)

Our platform is hosted on Vercel. Vercel may collect server logs including IP addresses and request metadata as part of their infrastructure operation. See: vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy

4. Cookies & Local Storage

APEX Intelligence uses no tracking cookies. We do not use cookies for advertising, user profiling, or cross-site tracking.

The platform may use browser sessionStorage or localStorage for:

  • Storing your selected timeframe preference within a session
  • Caching recently fetched price data to reduce API calls
  • Maintaining your asset filter selection

This data is stored entirely on your device, is never transmitted to our servers, and is cleared when you close your browser (sessionStorage) or clear your browser data (localStorage).

5. Advertising

APEX Intelligence displays advertising to support the free operation of the platform. We may use advertising networks including Google AdSense. Advertising networks may:

  • Use cookies to serve personalized advertisements based on your browsing history
  • Collect data about your interactions with advertisements
  • Use pixel tracking for ad measurement

You can opt out of personalized advertising through Google's Ad Settings at adssettings.google.com or through the Digital Advertising Alliance opt-out tool at optout.aboutads.info.

We comply with GDPR and CCPA requirements for advertising consent where applicable. Users in the European Economic Area will be presented with appropriate consent mechanisms.

6. Analytics

We may use privacy-focused analytics tools to understand aggregate usage of the platform. Any analytics we employ will be configured to:

  • Anonymize IP addresses before storage
  • Not share data with third-party advertising networks
  • Collect only aggregate, non-personally-identifiable statistics
  • Comply with applicable data protection regulations
